## Evaluation Criteria

Your peer feedback itself will be graded as part of the Peer Feedback Quality component (5% of the portfolio grade). Your feedback will be assessed on the following dimensions:

| Criterion | What "Excellent" Looks Like |
|---|---|
| **Specificity** | References specific sections, decisions, or language in the artifact; avoids generalities that could apply to any submission. |
| **Actionability** | Every suggestion includes a concrete recommendation for what the peer could do differently, add, or change. |
| **Balance** | Identifies genuine strengths (not just politeness) and genuine areas for improvement (not just nitpicks); both sides are substantive. |
| **Timeliness** | Submitted by the stated deadline, giving your peer adequate time to incorporate feedback before their final portfolio submission. |

**Remember:** The goal of peer feedback is to help your classmate produce the strongest possible portfolio. Be honest, be specific, and be constructive. The best feedback is the kind you would want to receive yourself.
