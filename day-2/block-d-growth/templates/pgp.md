## Evaluation Criteria

Your PGP will be assessed on the following dimensions:

| Criterion | Excellent | Satisfactory | Needs Revision |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **Specificity** | Strengths and growth areas are concrete, evidence-based, and unique to this person | Mostly specific but some areas are generic | Vague labels without evidence ("good communicator," "needs to be more strategic") |
| **Actionability** | Every development action has a clear timeline, measurement method, and support structure | Most actions are actionable; some lack timelines or measures | Actions are aspirational but not concrete enough to execute |
| **Realism** | Plan is ambitious but achievable within 6 months given the role and context | Mostly realistic; one or two actions may be overly ambitious | Plan is either too easy (no stretch) or too ambitious (sets up failure) |
| **Ownership** | Plan clearly positions the individual as the owner, with the manager as a supporter | Ownership is somewhat clear | Plan reads as something imposed by the manager rather than owned by the individual |
| **Case Context Fit** | Plan is clearly anchored to the specific case context (small/medium/large org) | Some connection to case context | Plan is generic and could apply to any organization |
